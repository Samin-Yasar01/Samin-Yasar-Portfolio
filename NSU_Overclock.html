NSU Overclock

About the Game

NSU Overclock is a groundbreaking third-person shooter game and Bangladesh's first story-based original RPG, set in a futuristic reimagining of real-life locations. The game places players in the role of a lone SWAT operative tasked with restoring order amidst chaos.

Game Features

Engaging Storyline

In the year 2050, North South University was hosting its traditional project showcase, Capstone. The excitement turned into chaos when a humanoid robot, developed by the university, gained self-awareness during its grand unveiling. Taking control of other robots and declaring independence, the robot activated a portal to another dimension, leaving behind destruction and a guardian robot to prevent pursuit. The elite SWAT team must step in to neutralize the threat and restore order.

Advanced AI Villains

  • A brawler with powerful melee combat.
  • A boxing AI exhibiting tactical movements.
  • A high-level bot with strategic behavior.

Meticulous Design

Maps, characters, and levels are crafted to align seamlessly with the storyline.

Dynamic Gameplay Mechanics

Experience a blend of tactical combat and strategic decision-making.

Cutting-edge Visuals

Built using Unreal Engine 5, the game showcases stunning graphics and immersive environments.

Innovation

NSU Overclock sets a new standard for locally inspired game development by celebrating the creativity and innovation of Bangladesh's gaming industry. This project not only marks the first-ever game development submission for the NSU Innovation Challenge (IC) but also stands as a testament to the potential of the country's emerging gaming community.

Development Details

  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Genre: Third-person shooter with RPG elements
  • Target Platform: PC
